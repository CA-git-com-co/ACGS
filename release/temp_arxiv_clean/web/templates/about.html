About This Tool

The Academic Submission Tool is a comprehensive validation system designed to help researchers prepare high-quality academic papers for submission to journals, conferences, and preprint servers like arXiv. Our tool automates the tedious process of checking submission requirements and identifying common issues that could delay publication.

How It Works

  1. Upload: Submit your paper files through our secure web interface
  2. Analyze: Our validation engine performs comprehensive checks
  3. Report: Receive detailed feedback with specific recommendations
  4. Improve: Address identified issues and re-validate as needed

Privacy & Security

We take your research privacy seriously. All uploaded files are processed locally and automatically deleted after validation. We do not store, share, or analyze your research content. The tool operates entirely on your submitted files without external data transmission.

Open Source

This tool is open source and available for academic use. We welcome contributions from the research community to improve validation capabilities and add support for additional venues and requirements.
